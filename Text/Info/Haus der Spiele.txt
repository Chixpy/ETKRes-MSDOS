Das Haus der Spiele is a collection of different games.

- DUELL (checkers) 
- MEMORY (think game)
- H\u00d6HER STICHT (card play game)
- PATIENCE (card play game)
- PUZZLE (think game)
- COLORS (think game)
- SCHEITERHAUFEN (game at dice)
- LOGIKA (4 little think games)
- TREFFER (game od reaction)  
- HASENLAUF (checkers)

But you are not alone in the house. Also other players try its luck, finally you play about money.
Try to reach the first place in the Highscore list and to get the money from the other players.

Para que haya sonido, entrar a la nota musical, desactivar el sonido, y volverlo a activar.
