Superfrog is a one of these rare traditional jump & run games. You\u2019re \u2013 surprise \u2013 a frog. Your enemies are all animals \u2013 like bees, hedgehogs or snails. You have no weapons. The only way to kill the enemies is to jump on them. Every stage has a timelimit. In this limit you have to collect a pretended number of coins.

Each of the 6 worlds has 4 stages. Every stage has a password. After all you can type your name into a hi score table.
