Magic Crayon is a children"s coloring book game where you get to color more than 30 pictures. You can color in four different parts:  Shading, Cartoon Critters, Coloring Book and Learning color. 

In Shading you look at a picture of a animal or plant and try to see if you can find it by coloring the background made with a lot of holes. The other modes allow playing and coloring freely.
