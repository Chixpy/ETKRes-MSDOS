The Walls of Bratock is a fantasy role-playing game that features some of the best VGA graphics ever seen on the shareware market, it also features music that will transport your soul to Bratock. The professionnal quality cinematic storyline will put you right into the game.  Converse with dozens of inhabitants, fight hordes of monsters, and explore endless areas as you try to avoid a war between the humans and their once allies Goat-men.
