A very short point and click "adventure" game.  You attempt to meet and seduce a young red-headed woman named "Vida".

The graphics in this game are (for the most part) digitized still pictures of an actual model; while sexual in nature, they never rise above the level of softcore.

The gameplay is incredibly simplistic, and it can be very hard to make a wrong move- typically, if you make an error, the game just gives a brief quip regarding why a move didn"t work, and then returns you to the exact same point to try again.
