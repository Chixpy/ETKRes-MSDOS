One of the few games set in exotic Arabia, Talisman combines the well-known Arabian myths of genies and evil vizors into a captivating game.As a Persian hero, find the legendary Talisman to destroy the evil genie who is ravaging the land. Puzzles are logical but made more memorable by their Arabian references. The parser is simple by adequate, and the storyline is captivating to the end. Thumbs up!
