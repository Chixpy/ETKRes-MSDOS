A very long time ago, in a distant galaxy, lived a man. People usually liked to call him "Ben", because that was his name. Ben always used his computer to design and write interesting games, but one day a very strange thing happened. He had just finished working on a game called "Wastoid" when a freak lightning storm sent a massive power surge into his computer, permanently melting his brain into the ancient PC-XT clone he was working with. Ever since that day, Ben has been trapped inside this game as a yellow bar of light, by that cruel, horrific, TWIST OF FATE...
