To play the game one must know four main actions of mathematics: addition, subtraction, multiplication and division and that the operations of multiplication and division will be done before the operations of addition or subtraction.

The game has seven levels for playing and levels two through seven are protected by passwords. 
Therefore, before you can play the next level, you must complete the previous level and be given the password.

Remember to write down the passwords so that you don"t forget them!!!

The difficulty of each level can be varied by choosing the options "Beginner", "Master", or "Expert". The options do not require passwords.
