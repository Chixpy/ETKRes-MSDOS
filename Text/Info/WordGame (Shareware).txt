"This is a simple word find game... when you start the game you have 3 choices..

A - Enter Words

You will have 5 minutes to find all the words in the maze. Simply type the word and hit enter. The game stops when you have found all the words or time runs out.

You may hit Q followed by enter to quit during this phase.

B - Computer Solve

If you choose this option the computer will find all the words in the maze and print out the solved maze.

Q - Quit"
