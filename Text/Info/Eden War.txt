This is a simple Taiwanese RTS with very interesting story. Just after his creation Adam had given a slap in the face to Eve (O.M.G!) and War in the Eden had begun! You can choose the side: men or\u2026 women. Yes, that\u2019s right, it\u2019s really bloody gender wars: WOMEN vs MEN! You will be as a commander (another words, you are in the God"s role) for squads with your man or woman fighters. Their wear is animal skins, and their weapons are heavy bludgeons. They will be fight and construct various building, of course, accordingly with the Lithic Age "architecture" rules. Your enemies are very strong, they are using even pterodactyls as aviation units! It"s a good RTS!
