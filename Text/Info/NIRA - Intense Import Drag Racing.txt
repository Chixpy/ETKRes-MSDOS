NIRA: Intense Import Drag Racing is a drag racing simulator. Tune your choice of 10 different cars and take it out to one of the several available game modes: single race, quick race, multi-race event or a full career mode.

Including over 20 race tracks and literally hundreds of parts you can customize your car with, if the single play modes aren"t enough, the game supports up to 32 players multi play modes.
