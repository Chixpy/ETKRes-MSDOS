The player once again steps into the, er, thong of meaty galactic hero Brad Stallion, this time aiming to thwart the schemes of Dr. Dildo by rescuing Princess Orgasma of the pleasure planet Erotica, again with the help of handy shipboard computer Sandie.  The lessons learned in the first game are reiterated here -- mainly, when to take Brad"s clothes off and when to put them back on.

This is still accomplished through a text parser, feeling your way around the world by scrutinizing brief descriptions and static, non-interactive screens colourfully illustrating available exits and the objects of your interest and desire.
