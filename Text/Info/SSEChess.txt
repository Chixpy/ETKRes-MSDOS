To start a game of chess against SSEChess, simply type SSECHESS and hit Enter in the directory where SSECHESS.EXE is located.  The computer will randomly decide who is white and who is black.  To get a list of command line parameters with descriptions type "SSECHESS /?" and hit ENTER.  Default values are : Time Per Move - 30 seconds, Maximum Search Depth - 20 plies, Book - On, Keyboard - On (Mouse - Off), Color - Random, and Wild - Off.
