Stone Mist 2 is a 3/4 perspective, animated VGA role playing game. The world is called Stone Mist, a huge medieval domain, similar in technological achievements accomplished during Earths own Middle Ages.  All of Stone Mists gods, legends and lore are based on the actual beliefs and superstitions of the people during the Middle Ages.  A heavy dose of Norse mythology is also present in the cultures of Stone Mist. 

Stone Mist is an entity, its own world, a world wracked by violence looking for a hero.
