Elite Plus is a VGA re-make of Bell and Braben"s classic 3D space trading flight simulator, Elite.

It is an updated conversion of the original that features new graphics and sounds/music.

Elite Plus included a different novella than the original Elite releases.

Elite Plus included the story "Imprint" by Andy Redman, which formed the latter part of the manual while Elite included "The Dark Wheel" by Robert Holdstock as a separate book.
