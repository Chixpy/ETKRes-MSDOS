Advanced Dungeons & Dragons (Collector"s Edition) contains all nine original Gold Box AD&D games on one CD-ROM. Complete hard copy documents for all of the games are also included. Games contained on the disc are:

Champions of Krynn
Curse of the Azure Bonds
The Dark Queen of Krynn
Death Knights of Krynn
Gateway to the Savage Frontier
Pools of Darkness
Pool of Radiance
Secret of the Silver Blades
Treasures of the Savage Frontier
