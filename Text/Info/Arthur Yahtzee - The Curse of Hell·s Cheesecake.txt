In a secret base far underneath Washington DC, decisions are made.  Decisions that can - and will - affect the entire world.  This is the base of the Special Secret Service, a worldwide organization outside of petty international disputes, assigned to protect our planet at whatever cost from those things the general public are better off being ignorant of.  On this particular day, a collection of the most important and least known people on the planet sat around the huge varnished conference table...
