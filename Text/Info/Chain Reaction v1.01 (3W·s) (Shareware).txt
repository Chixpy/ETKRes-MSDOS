The cells are divided into three kinds: The Corner cells, the Side cells, and the Inner cells.

Player can only place up to 2 seeds in each of the corner cells, 3 seeds in each of the side cells and up to 4 seeds in each of the inner cells

Each player will take turns to place their moves. (NOTE: Player move are being notified by the white colored box in their respective name.)

The first person who dominate the whole game board with their own types of color seeds is the winner of the game.
