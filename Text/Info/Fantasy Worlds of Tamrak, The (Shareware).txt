You  are  an ingenious  adventurer  who  has  perfected a time portal. The portal is disguised as part of the wall so as not to be detected by visitors to your lab.

In this current adventure through time you are  carried  back to  The  Worlds  of  Tamrak. In these worlds you will have to locate  12  jewels  to be embedded in the Icon of Tamrak. For only when all these jewels are returned to there proper place will they  be able to  radiate  forth  the  powers  that  lay within.  As  to what these powers are.  We will have  to wait and see.
