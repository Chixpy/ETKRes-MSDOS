Another Wizard Works compilation cash-in. this time an add-on for id"s Quake.

The new campaign has no plot and is simply a collection of 35 new single-player levels sprawled over three new episodes, plus a series of deathmatch / capture the flag maps. Included are four new monsters and a new weapon; the slicer, which is based on the nailgun and shoots icy shurikens.
