An update of the previous Gazillionaire space-trading game. Gazillionaire Deluxe includes the existing gameplay of that title and offers the following changes and updates:

- Ability to customize the solar system from a set of pre-existing planets

- There are twice as many spaceships to choose from

- Double the number of "random event" travel encounters

- A new Stock Markets system that lets players invest on a particular planet

- Adjustable intelligence for the computer players

- Play-by-email functionality for multiplayer games
