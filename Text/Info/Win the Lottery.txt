This package is designed for anyone who enjoys playing the National Lottery.  A very simple and easy to use package. From the lottery menu just select an option and follow the prompts.

The computer can pick a line of balls using a graphical display, or if you are in a syndicate the computer will pick as many as 50 lines of numbers.  These numbers can then be printed out.

An extra feature is the Hot Numbers option, by saving previously drawn numbers in a file the computer will find the Hot Numbers.
