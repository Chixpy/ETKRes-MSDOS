Take a Break! Pinball contains a set of 8 pinball tables based upon various popular Sierra games:

Larry"s Big Score (Leisure Suit Larry series)
Lots in Space (Draxon: Level 2 - Nova 9: Return of Gir Draxon)Flipped Out Willy (The Adventures of Willy Beamish)
Nova 9 (Draxon: Level 1 - Nova 9: Return of Gir Draxon)
Planet Xenon in the Beginning (Planet Pinball: Level 1 - Space Quest series)
Quest for Daventry (King"s Quest series)
Reformation Day (Planet Pinball: Level 3 - Space Quest series)
Spaced Travel (Planet Pinball: Level 2 - Space Quest series)
