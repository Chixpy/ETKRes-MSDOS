U.S. Navy Fighters allows players to fly US naval aircraft over Ukraine in a war against Russia. The game features a campaign, single missions, an in-game single mission creator, and a quick mission creator.

"Marine Fighters" is an expansion disk for US Navy Fighters, it adds VSTOL aircraft such as the Harrier and Yak-141, plus the carriers that support them (USS Wasp). Includes more aircraft, more vehicles, bug fixes, plus an extra map and campaign (Kurile Islands)

The Gold release features the flight simulator U.S. Navy Fighters and its add-on, Marine Fighters.
