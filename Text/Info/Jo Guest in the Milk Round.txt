Jo Guest in the Milk Round is another Interactive Girl Club game featuring a well-known British model: Joanne Guest. The game is played as a mouse-driven adventure and the plot has much in common with other IGC titles. The player, as a sudden substitute for his milkman friend, has to deliver milk in the local neighborhood. At one of the houses he is invited inside by a young woman. As the game is progressing the hostess reveals more and more about herself, until a "victorious" sex-scene happens.
