The sequel to Wolfmods I through III, Strange Worlds just popped out, inspired by the loose ends and leftover ideas that were formulated in the development of the Wolfmods series. All new maps, it carries you through the worlds of Blakestein, Doomstein, Pacstein and Romanstein, in a thrilling breathtaking adventure. 24 individual games, a choice of 5 sets of graphics, full-screen capacity, bobbing weapons, stunning 3D worlds, all make this a special game.
