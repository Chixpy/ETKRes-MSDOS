"A short text based game where you are (usually) given a series of choices like a choose your own adventure book. In the game you play a character in his first day at a new high school. The game ranks you on how many friends you"ve made, money you have, enemies, and GPA.

The choices and storyline change depending on your choices - but there is also some randomness in the game. Even choosing the same things time and time again will sometimes have different plot branches.

Check out the games website for some interesting history and tips (last updated in 2000)."
