Invading forces from foreign galaxies, the personal logs of Dr. Vladimir Rovinski, and unexplored quadrants in outer space lend intrigue and excitement to Galaxy Trek! 640x480 Graphics, Polyphonic sound, Roland/Soundblaster support. An excellent game; compare it to the best you have. Requires 386+, VGA, HIMEM.SYS or equivalent upper memory manager, mouse, and 3 MB RAM.
