Cave Raider is an amazing ASCII adventure game that is a lot of fun. You are a young man or a young woman and enter a dangerous underground cave system with six consecutive caves. You"ll find a lot of gold in these caves, but also terrible cave monsters that guard the gold. Your intention is to raid
cave after cave, get all the gold and leave the cave system alive. The layout of the individual caves is randomly generated. This means that each game will offer you a new challenge! In addition, you can choose between three difficulty settings (Easy, Normal and Hard). If you have some spare time, then give this game a try. The best cave raiders will get an entry in the Hall of Fame.
