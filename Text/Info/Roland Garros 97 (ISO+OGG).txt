RG97 is a tennis game with 3D characters.  The game takes its name from a real famous French tennis tournament, but the characters in the game are made up.  You can choose from between 10 different males, 7 females, or 3 futuristic players.

You can play on 5 grounds (two are hidden and need to be unlocked) which are Roland Garros (France), England, USA, Australia, and Power Dome (a futuristic one).

You can play single or double matches, and you have the choice between 3 difficulty levels. The four different game modes include Single match, Network, Friendly match, and Tournament.You can also choose the camera angle to play with (look at the screenshots).
