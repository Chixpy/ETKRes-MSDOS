WUNDER BOOK is a collection of 5 colorful and fun games for children 3 to 10 years old to learn and explore "wunderful" objects, numbers, alphabets, words, shapes, and 4 different languages (English, Spanish, French, and German) using the universal language of graphics. 40 pictures are included. The games are totally child-friendly, symbol-menu-driven, supports (optional) mouse, music, and scores.       

Great games! Additional pictures and libraries can be added.
