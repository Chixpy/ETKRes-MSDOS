Video Keno can be found in many of the major gambling establisments throughout the world. Video Keno is played by marking from 1 to 15 numbers from a field of 80. The player then inserts from 1 to 5 coins. A total 20 numbers are then selected at random. The object is to select the numbers that will be drawn. The amount a player may win will depend on the amount bet and the total amount of numbers that have been selected.
