EGAWHEEL is a shareware "Wheel of Fortune"/hangman type word game for IBM compatible computers requiring EGA. The game features: a colorful spinning wheel, 1-3 players, 5 rounds of play, 1000 puzzles, mouse control, a sound on/off toggle option, automatic win after all the letters have been bought and guessed, a high score table for the top ten WHEELERS, and an editor to add/delete/modify puzzles. Registered users receive over 12,000 puzzles.  Registration is $10 in U.S. funds. The author is a member of the Association of Shareware Professionals.
