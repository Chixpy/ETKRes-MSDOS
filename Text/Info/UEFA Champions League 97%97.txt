UEFA Champions League 1996/1997 is a soccer simulation licensed by European soccer association UEFA. It features the 16 teams of the 1996/1997 soccer championship Champions League and additionally 32 other teams which can be played in other fictional championship modes, e.g. a league. Thanks to the license it features the real player names, digitalized photos and the original stadiums.

The matches itself are mostly action oriented. The player can perform several tricks and the standard soccer actions like shooting and passing. The game features several camera angles.
