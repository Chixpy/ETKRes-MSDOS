"WW III: Thermonuclear Warfare is a nuclear war simulation in which you have control over where your missiles are launched from and what they hit. The nuclear war in this game is waged between the North Atlantic Treaty Organization (NATO), which consists of the U.S.A. and its allies, and the Warsaw Treaty Organization (WTO), or Warsaw Pact, which consists of the U.S.S.R. and its allies. The object of the game is to have the margin between deaths on your side and deaths on the enemy"s side the largest.

This game supports both single player and two players."
