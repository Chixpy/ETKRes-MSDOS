Battle of the Elements is two games in one. The primary game is similar in general concept to Othello. 1 to 4 players can play as various elements and forces of nature (10 to choose from) and test their strategic thinking against one another or against the computer. The secondary game is a puzzle game with 70 boards to play. Features (as usual) full color VGA, and soundblaster digital sound. VGA, Mouse, 400k+ RAM, and 286+ processor required.
