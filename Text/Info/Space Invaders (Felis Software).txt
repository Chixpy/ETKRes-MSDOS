About Invaders :

Coded in pure ASM for VGA resolution. 

Sound support : PC speaker ( digitalized music ), Sound Blaster compatible, COVOX on LPT1,2 ( D/A ), GUS stereo.

Hardware requirements : 286 or better, VGA or SVGA card, 400 KB hard space, keyboard or joystick

We released shareware - demo version in beginig of 1995. This summer support for sound cards was included and bugs fixed.
