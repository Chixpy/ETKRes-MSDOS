Plunder & Pillage is a total conversion for Duke Nukem 3D. It was created by Petrilla Enterprises and released on March 24th, 2002. It includes new levels, new weapons, new enemy sprites, new sound effects, new music, and new voiceovers. The only version known to be released is a demo (ver. 1.0) of the mod; it is unknown if Plunder & Pillage was ever completed.
