In Gloria, the player controls a British exchange student with a good reputation in an American university, soon employed by a famous millionaire who wants the player to be a tutor for his daughters. He has five of them, all living in a beautiful mansion. Now it"s up to the player to educate them... 

The game is a standard Japanese-style adventure. The player interacts with the environments by selecting text commands from a menu. There are no puzzles, but occasional choices made by the player, which might influence subsequent events. The game has 45 possible endings.
