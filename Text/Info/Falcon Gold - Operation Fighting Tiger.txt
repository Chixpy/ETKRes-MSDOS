A massive compilation from Spectrum Holobyte, Falcon Gold includes updated versions of Falcon 3.0 (version 3.05) and its three expansions, and the video documentary on dogfighting, Art of the Kill. 

The expansions are:

MiG-29 (version 1.04)
Hornet: Naval Strike Fighter (version 1.02)
Operation: Fighting Tiger (version 1.2)
