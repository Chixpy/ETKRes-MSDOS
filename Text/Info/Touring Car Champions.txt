Touring Car Champions was the first attempt to create a game based on The Australian Touring Car Championship and Bathurst 1000. It was also the first to use Full Motion Video as the base for a racing game. Only 1 track (Bathurst) is included. Touring Car legends was fully endorsed by the Holden Racing Team, Holden Special Vehicles, Mount Panorama Consortium, Mt Panorama Motor Racing Hall of Fame.
