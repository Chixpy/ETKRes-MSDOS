Fight"n Jokes tells the adventures of Jokes, a group of fighters who combat to free their planet from an evil entity.

The game is a 2D beat "em up with toon-style graphic and sound. It features ten playable characters (like Miopninja, a ninja with glasses, Arturo, a tribute to Ghosts "N Goblins hero, or Lampaddin, similar to Aladdin"s Genie) plus 4 unlockables; a chain combo system; digital audio-cd tracks; final moves called "Dementiality". Graphics are hand-drawn and every characters has about 300 frames of animations.
