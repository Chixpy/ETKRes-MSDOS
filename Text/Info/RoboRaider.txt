RoboRaider puts you in control of 6 different robots to explore 6 different mission including 3 test of skills. Although it has limited graphics, it has a unfolding RPG game play that should prove interesting at most. So grab the keyboard, and take on this 
MiniRPG!

PS: Be careful of Dr. Robo, he loves his robots, and will fire you if any harm comes to them. Pilot them with care!

Features:

320 x 200 Graphics screen size (At least its easy to read :P)

16 Colors

6 Robots (All are used)

9 Levels plus a bonus to unlock

xtrGRAPHICS(TM) Level 1 (Uses QBasic"s graphic engine)
{Classic Style Game}

TPT(Third Person Top) Veiw
