"The object of this game is to make as much money as possible. Your uncle has entrusted you to take over his successful oil well in Texas. Your objective is to use your keen business skills in the importing and exporting of oil products. Your uncle has given you $1,000,000, a refinery, and one oil well to start your venture with.

In addition to the items given to you by your uncle, he has requested that your only run the business for the next ten years (120 months). He has not given you any hints of the trade but he has made a list of suggestions for you..."
