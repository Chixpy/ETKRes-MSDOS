SuperBlox is a 256-color VGA adaptation of Tetris.  Colorful graphics spice up a great game.  New features include:

o Bombs!
Blast away blocks in the playing field.  Bombs away!

o Dynamic Playing Field Option!    
Placed blocks randomly move up or down to add more challenge.

o Self-Play Mode!  
Watch the computer play SuperBlox.

oTextured Block Styles!
As the levels increase, the block elements are drawn with new textures and patterns.

o Animations!      
You"ll have to see for yourself.

o Configureable!   
Change the starting level, allow/disallow bombs, toggle dynamic playing field option, turn on/off sounds, and more.  All of these options can be changed during the game.
