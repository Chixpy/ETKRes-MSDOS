This is an interactive computer Role-Playing Game. Although there are no graphics in this game, it is still a very enjoyable game to play. During your travels in QUEST, you will be able to use many different types of weapons, armors, potions, and items. Also, there are several secrets & surprises hidden in QUEST.



THE STORY (so far):

DARK LORD, the archvillain, has taken over the five worlds that make up the universe.

You are a brave warrior. Every day, you and everyone else must live in fear of DARK LORD"s monsters.

One day, you decide that you"ve had enough, and you set out to defeat DARK LORD and his army of monsters. You take a dagger and some cash with you..

...and the QUEST begins!
