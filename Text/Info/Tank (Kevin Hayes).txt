GAME KEYS:

ESC - hit this to quit the game
F1  - toggles invisiblity game OFF\ON
F2  - toggles the type of wall NORMAL\BOUNCING\WARP
F3  - toggles teleport mode ON\OFF

Player 1
  colors:  GREEN  - systems are O.K.
           YELLOW - use caution
           RED    - danger!!!

  w  - accelerate
  s  - decelerate
  a  - rotate counter-clockwise
  d  - rotate clockwise
  m  - fire a missle
  n  - teleport (if in use)

Player 2
  colors:  CYAN    - systems are O.K.
           WHITE   - use caution
           MAGENTA - danger!!

  8  - accelerate
  5  - decelerate
  4  - rotate couter-clockwise
  6  - rotate clockwise
  /  - fire a missle
  .  - teleport (if in use)
