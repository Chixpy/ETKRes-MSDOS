FLIP-N-DROP is a game that will provide the User countless hours of enjoyment. This program is similar to the well known game, Tetris; but is different enough to enjoy play-both games. There are three possible Wins per game with a total of ten different playing levels to really make the game exciting and challenging. Great graphics and sound effects, records Top Ten Winners and is played with the four Arrow Keys on the Keyboard. Easy to use from Computer Specialties.
