WORDSEARCH/BINGO POWER will create and print out word searches, vocabulary bingo cards and flash cards from your words and definitions. The word search size can be varied up to 17 columns and 17 rows. Below the word search, the hidden word, a definition of the word, or the first letter of the hidden word with trailing blanks can be printed. A variety of VOCABULARY BINGO CARDS can also be created and printed from your list of vocabulary words. The BINGO calling cards can also be used as FLASH CARDS to learn vocabulary. The word lists created are 100% compatible with our other product CROSSWORD POWER. This allows these vocabulary words to be automatically arranged in crossword puzzles with their definitions printed below.
