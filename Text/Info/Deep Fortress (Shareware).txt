"You are an elite helicopter pilot chosen by the high command officials to infiltrate an underground fortress, rescue all of captured high command officers, and exterminate the evil terrorist. The fortress contains seven tightly guarded bunkers. Each bunker contains shield generators for the next bunker. You"ll have to destroy all shield generators in each of the bunker and survive the self-destruct explosion. Each bunker is guarded by enemy aircraft, vehicles, weapons, and mind-boggling security systems.

To win the game, you must rescue the captured high command officials, destroy all seven bunkers, and kill the evil terrorist."
