Orel Hershiser"s Strike Zone is a action-oriented baseball game starring the baseball star Orel Hershiser.

This game offers 2 modes of play - strike zone (main game), and home run derby (batting training).This gameplay is made up for by animations of baseball players, and user interfacethat allows you to adjust both the speed and direction of your bat with on-screen gaugesThe player can choose among 26 teams and play directly in the positions of batter and pitcher.A managerial element is also included: when playing a whole season the player can choose the lineup and the batting order.
