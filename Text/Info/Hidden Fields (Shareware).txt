Hidden Fields is an action-strategy game. Published by LOMAX SOFTWARE (VGA/386sx25). It features super 256 color graphics and brilliant sound effects ( SB or 100% compatible ). The game contains 28 mind blasting level with lots of enemies. Hidden Fields is total FUN guaranteed. You must control your heart through levels with dangerous monsters which you must catch or destroy. If you catch your monster in a box the rest of the play field must be min. 90% of the whole. Also you can shoot the monster if you could collect a bonus field.
