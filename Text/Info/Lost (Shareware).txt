Lost is a 3-D environmental game. This UNREGISTERED version of Lost, only has 1 [one] level of play. The full REGISTERED [Commercial] version, contains all 5 levels. In this shareware version of Lost, you must collect and find all 5 gold coins in order to leave the MAZE to enter a higher level of the maze. You must get to Level 5 and save a crystal engery capsule.
