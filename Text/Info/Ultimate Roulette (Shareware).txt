From 12 animated characters up to 4 members can play with your character at a 5 seat table. Choose European wheel with green 0 or the standard double 0 wheel. House advantage possible bets payoff and actual odds are displayed to each bet. A simulation feature allows you to quickly see the results of your bet strategy with up to 30000 spins of the wheel. Every possible bet is implemented. On line help is available for table rules and bet placement. Soundblaster voice files and sound effects available to registered users.
