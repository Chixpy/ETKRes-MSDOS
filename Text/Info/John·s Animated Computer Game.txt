"""John"s Animated Computer Game"" is an easy to use game for very young children to develop an acquaintance with the computer.

20 different animated sequences appear on the screen to entertain young ones at the touch of a key. This program was written so that my 2 year old could have a game that he could play by himself.

""John"s Animated Computer Game"" was written by an artist with over 15 years" experience in the field of classical animation."
