Corncob 3-D: The Other Worlds Campaign is an alternate version of the original Corncob 3-D which could be purchased directly from the manufacturer.

After successfully driving out the alien invasion in the original game, the aliens have taken refuge in the other planets of the solar system! They"re definitely planning a comeback, but the governments of the world have joined forces again, this time to drive them out of our solar system!

Part sequel, part expansion, the game is now set in six different alien planets. It also has these new features:

130 new missions
A mission and sound editor
Expanded documentation and an extensive level creation tutorial
In-game score and stat reports
