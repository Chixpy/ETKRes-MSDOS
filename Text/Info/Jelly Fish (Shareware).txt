A fast-paced arcade game in which the player takes the role of a Jellyfish who lives in great danger. To gain points the Jellyfish must collect bubbles and gold coins while avoiding entanglement in seaweed. If the Jellyfish succeeds in staying alive sea monsters soon begin appearing to make the game even more challenging! This game will provide you with hours of fun. Use of joystick or keyboard. Requires CGA.
