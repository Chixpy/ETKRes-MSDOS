The best and most realistic horse racing simulation seen.  Point and click your way through the entire game with your mouse.  Review the Daily Racing form before placing a bet and see the horses past racing fortunes.  Watch the race as it happens from an overhead view then review the video of the race after wards.  1000 horses in 10 stables are provied to keep the action fresh.  Win, Place, Show, Quennella, Exacta, and Daily Double bets are all available.
