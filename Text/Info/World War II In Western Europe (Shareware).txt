1 or 2 player strategy level game of World War II in Europe, based on the successful VGA Civil War Strategy game approach. Includes over 50 key city locations, 5 army types (infantry, armor, artillery, elite, and static) and air force and naval capabilities. Uses 8 distinctive technologies to upgrade combat and production. Has Play by Mail feature. Includes multiple scenarios. Has user-accessible combat and other parameters. Weather and other factors included. Recruit armies, conduct air strikes and naval bombardments, build fortifications, make paratroop drops, conduct invasions, and improve technology. Game has online help and over 30 pages of documentation. Easy to begin to play, but has depth, features, and flexibility to make it a continuing favorite.
