Fritz Deluxe is a training tool for chess players of all levels. It includes tutorials and other in-game training aids to help you get the most out of your games and learn from your mistakes. With an ELO rating of 2200 points the AI is strong enough to be challenging players up to candidate master level. More than 500 games of chess history were included which you can learn from to increase your own gameplay.
