When the news of Bill Cosby"s death in the town of Reality-on-the-Norm shake the nation, Mika Huy - a tabloid photographer - arrives into Reality in order to make a photo of Bill"s corpse. But on her way, she comes across some most peculiar individuals and things, and the discovery of Bill Cosby"s body might be more breathtaking than she expected...

The game is controlled with a mouse; similarly to Sam & Max Hit The Road, right-clicking changes the cursor action (Walk to, Look at, Interact with, Talk to), while moving the cursor to the top of the screen makes a menu appear, allowing you to easily change the cursor actions, access one"s inventory or save/load/quit the game.
