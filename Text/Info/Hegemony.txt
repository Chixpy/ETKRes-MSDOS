"Hegemony 3.0.e" is a turn-based strategy game that simulates the hegemonic efforts of medieval empires. "The game of the middle ages" requires correct decisions in fields of economics, science, diplomacy, and military. Finding a healthy balance among these fields is essential to achive the final goal: 

The establishment of a perfect hegemony. 

A goal that so many empires pursued unsuccesfully in history. Now, it"s your turn to rewrite history...
