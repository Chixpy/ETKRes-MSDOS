It is the year 3067 and humans have expanded to the stars. The known universe is broken up into 10 different governments, all in conflict with one another. They have adopted names in accordance with their home-worlds. One planet in particular, Kintares IV, Is right at the intersection of all 10 territories. Here is the ideal place from which to launch attacks, and the owner of the planet would have absolute control over inter-territory trade. Several of these factions have thus sent a ship with combat robots on board and one person to lead them. This is where the game begins.
