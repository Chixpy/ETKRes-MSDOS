In "The Hunt", you take control of "Malcolm Rhodes", an Ex-Army Driver who has been falsely convicted of murder. Malcolm has managed to steal the judge"s car, and the game begins in a high speed pursuit as he makes a desperate attempt to evade capture. On his journey, Malcom will be chased by police cars, intercepted at roadblocks, struggle to avoid hitting regular drivers and perhaps even come face to face with an army tank...
