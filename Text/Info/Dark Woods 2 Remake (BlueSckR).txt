Dark Woods 2 Remake is a small strategy game, based on Jocke The Beast"s Dark Woods 2. The whole game concept is copied, with Jocke"s permission, beside a few additions:

-Graphics (Dark Woods 2 is an ASCI(I) game)
-Night/Day modes
-Monster can be displayed as "ghosts" or lighten up, darken...
-particles
-A Level-Pack Selector
-New "You failed/You made it" screens
-Weather effects
-A Highscore table
-A custom level selector (!!!)
