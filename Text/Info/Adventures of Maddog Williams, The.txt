...in the Dungeons of Duridian (for the complete title)

King Serak of the land Duridian was a good king for the people, until an evil high priest began to influence his mind and turned him bad. He enslaved people and forced them to built an new castle.

Thaylor and his brave men began a fight and won. They captures King Serak. Robert Thaylor became king.

Of course Serak returns and you are to help Maddog Williams to get rid of the evil spreading over the land.

Fun game!
