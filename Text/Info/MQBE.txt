MQBE is a puzzle based on Professor Ern\u201d Rubik"s Magic Cube (called B\u0081v\u201ds Kocka in Hungarian), the 20th century"s most amazing puzzle.
     
In the late 70s and early 80s the Cube became a world-wide fad.

While fads come and go, for some of us the Magic Cube remains as fascinating as ever.  After all, there are not too many puzzles that offer  43,252,003,274,489,856,000  patterns.  To have seen all possible patterns, if you could have viewed 1,000 patterns every second, you would have had to start when the universe was born.
