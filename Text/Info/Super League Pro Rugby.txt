Alternative Software have acquired the official licence to Super League.

In the game you choose one of Super League"s teams and play the match with another team. During match you are player with the ball. So you can give a ball to another player or run with the ball or kick the ball for scoring. Rules of rugby are unchanged.

Sky TV"s own commentary team of Eddie Hemmings and Mike Stephenson have been drafted in to provide the in-game vocals.
