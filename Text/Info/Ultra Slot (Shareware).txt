Ultra Slot is a slot machine gambling game allowing the user to select from a large variety of slot machines. You can play on a 3, 4 or 5 reel slot, and select from 1 to 5 pay lines. The best part of Ultra Slot is the ability to construct your own slot machine with your favorite symbols and payoffs. You can add or remove any symbol from any of the slot machines or even change the payoffs. You can do almost anything with Ultra Slot except cash in your winnings.
