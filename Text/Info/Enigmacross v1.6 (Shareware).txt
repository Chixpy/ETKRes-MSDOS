Here is another superb word game program from the author of CROSSDOWN. With ENIGMACROSS you can both solve and construct professional quality acrostic puzzles on your computer. The software is very easy to use featuring a colorful mouse-driven graphical interface animated selection buttons and sound effects. Acrostics are solved exactly like those in newspapers or magazines. Clearly written on-screen help guides you effortlessly through the construction process. For those who need hard copies separate printing utilities output puzzles in hi-res graphics with acrostic numbering and cross-keying done automatically. This shareware package contains the complete program 7 sample acrostics full documentation and drivers for most popular printers. ENIGMACROSS requires an EGA/VGA monitor a mouse 512K of RAM and DOS 3.2 or greater. Member: Association of Shareware Professionals (ASP).
