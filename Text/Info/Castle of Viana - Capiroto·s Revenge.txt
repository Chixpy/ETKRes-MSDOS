A #cgajam MS-DOS game  (theme: history repeats itself) where you traverse a floating castle in Viana do Castelo to kill the demon Capiroto (again).

How to play:

z - Jump

x - Shoot arrow

c - Attack

Up - Shoot arrow up/climb stairs

Arrows - Move

Esc - Quit

Enter - Pause
