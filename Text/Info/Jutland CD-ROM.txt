Command a fearsome dreadnought and fight for control of the North and South Atlantic as either the German Imperial Fleet or the British Royal Navy during WW I. Fight a single mission, a  campaign or the entire war while commanding 1 ship, a squadron of ships or the entire fleet. Experience night combat, torpedo attacks, salvos of exploding shells and burning ships. WW I naval combat comes to life with SVGA and VGA graphics, and with digitized video and sound effects. Over 100 missions to play.
