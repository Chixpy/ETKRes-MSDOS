This is the ultimate world art history education presented as an entertaining Trivia game. A mixture of interesting facts that combines art with historical, mythological and cultural beliefs, it is presented as both true-false or multiple choice questions. The purpose of art is to tell the story of our cultures, and the history of mankind. You can browse the data base before beginning play against your friends or yourself. Enjoy a truly educational game that will provide a lifetime of learning as you explore the tribal cultures and high civilizations of the globe.
