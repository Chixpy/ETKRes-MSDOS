Pong Worz is another variation of classic pong game.

This pong game introduces many new features into classic pong concept and offers several game modes.

You have 4 game modes to play including classic pong mode.
In Pong Worz you"ll be able to use weapons,control different type of paddles,play with two balls,pickup powerups and experience other interesting features like paddles moving with acceleration and inertia.
