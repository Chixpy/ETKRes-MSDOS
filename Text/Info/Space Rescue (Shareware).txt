"You are about to embark on a mission to a distant planet in urgent need of medical supplies. First, You must get your ship ready for the trip by allocating some of the ships energy to the engines, shields and life support. You are then put to sleep for the main part of the trip, after which you will get a report of the events on the way. You must then land on the planet!

Will you survive the trip?, or be destroyed in an asteroid storm? Will you land ok? or will you make a new crater or shooting star? Only one way to find out... Play the game...."
