You were thrilled to hear that a month stay was arranged for you in an old manor. For a horror novel author it was simply the ideal place to find inspiration and atmosphere for a new story, as the building itself had a story on its own: in the last 100 years there was an unusual number of murders and suicides committed here, the last owners disappeared 7 years ago. Who knows, maybe there is too much truth in those spooky tales after all, as your publicist who got you the keys for this place is still being hospitalized...

Hampton Manor is an interactive fiction horror with simple parser and the ability to save your progress. You"re given a score to help you track how far you are into the story.
