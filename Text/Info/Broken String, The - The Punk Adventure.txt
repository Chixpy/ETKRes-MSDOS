The Broken String, is a Punk-Rock Interactive Fiction (or text adventure). In it you play the role of Tim, a young punk rocker, living in an imaginary city in a squat. In Tim"s city punk rock is dying and along with it all the ideals that true punk represent. Tim"s feeling of frustration and alienation grow stronger and stronger every day, until they come to a blowing point. But instead of loosing hope, Tim decides to go on a crusade to revive punk. He intends to put up a band, have some gigs and make punk a threat again. Along the way Tim will encounter different problems, not all of them related in a direct manner to his task. Tim will also try to solve his personal problems as he goes on with his crusade. You must guide Tim through the city and help him complete his goal.
