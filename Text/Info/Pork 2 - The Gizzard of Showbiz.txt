This game is a sequel to Pork I.  Pork I was a parody of Infocom"s great text game, Zork I; Pork I was enhanced and rewritten in AGT format by Dave Malmberg of Softworks, creators of AGT.

The present game, Pork II, continues with tradition with a parody of Zork II, the sequel to Zork I.  This game has a TAME or LEWD mode of play.  The LEWD mode is not recommended for youngsters.
