"The evil SAD GRAPHICS MAN has captured the beautiful princess Mega Galactic Snake and has taken her to his secret lair. You have vowed to rescue the princess, promising yourself that you would not stop until you have succeeded or until you get bored, whichever comes first.

To get to the secret lair of Sad Graphics Man, you must travel on a journey through several tricky levels, eating a required amount of food on each level...

You must drive Ultra Peril Snake through these levels using the keyboard and eating the food. However, you cannot crash into any walls or into your tail and you cannot stop your snake. Also, your snake gets longer every time you eat some food, which makes the level harder as you progress. There are special blocks which you can eat to do special things- like shrink your body."
