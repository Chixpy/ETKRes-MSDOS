You are a detective, hired to protect Mr. Violet, a tycoon who lives in a mansion on a small New England island.  You catch a ferry to the island at 6:00 PM, but, too late!  When you arrive, you find Mr. Violet lying face down in his bedroom murdered.  The next ferry comes at 10:00 the next morning.  You have one night to solve the crime and arrest the criminal before he escapes.
