This collection includes the original Harpoon II, part of the long-running naval strategy series, and seven distinct battle sets:

Global Conflicts One (as included with the original game)
Global Conflicts Two
Global Conflicts Three
Cold War
Westpac
Regional Conflicts One
Regional Conflicts Two (exclusive to this pack)

Anything is possible in this game and with the built-in scenario editor you can create whatever type of battle you wish.
