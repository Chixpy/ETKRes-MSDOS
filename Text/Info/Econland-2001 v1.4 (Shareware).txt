ECONOLAND-2001 simulates economic policy!

Find out what happens to unemployment and inflation when you take charge of fiscal and monetary policy in ECONOLAND.  You set the level of government spending, tax rates and the rate of growth of the money supply.  Face up to OPEC, demobilization, 
and technological unemployment.  Program measures your EPQ (Economic Policy Quotient).  Challenge your friends to beat your top score.
