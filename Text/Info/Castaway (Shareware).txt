The Slrrian War has not gone well. The Terran Stellar Union was unprepared for the Slrrian assault. Many worlds, once terraformed into lush gardens and busy cities have been either occupied or annihilated altogether. The Slrrians began to clone many of the captured human prisoners in an effort to increase their number. (A typical Slrrian has only one offspring). The human clones are used to conquer Terran worlds where the Slrrians themselves would suffocate...
