Inspector Gadget: Safety Patrol is another title in the Electric Crayon series. The game is a coloring book with 30 illustrations, with the characters of the famous TV cartoon "Inspector Gadget" that include safety education messages.

There are 16 colors "in screen" for coloring the pictures, but the user has the "mix option" that can get 256 colors.

Later, pictures could be printed with, or without, a message, a monthly calendar etc.
