Yes, the game that started it all, Ambush at South Range! Easily one of,  if not THE, most creative QBasic game ever, Ambush at South Range is an addictive game that will keep you entertained for hours! Also difficult, you can request strategies via email at the addresses below. In Ambush at South Range, you plot to overtake the South Range middle school, and, in the ultimate battle, destroy Mr. Wuss himself. Ambush at South Range was created in August of 1994, and at that time Molnar \ Kucalaba Productions did not have half the understanding of the BASIC language that they do today, so we relied on our strong point: creativity. We hope you enjoy Ambush at South Range, and by all means, email us!
