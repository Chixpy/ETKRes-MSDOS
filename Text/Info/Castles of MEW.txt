Welcome to the Castles of MEW. Inside you will be exploring two MEW castles and caves of the MEW world. You will be looking for treasure, and attempting to slay the fearsome dragons who live in the caves.

You will start your adventure with 25 units of Charisma and you must complete your task before the Charisma is worn out. You lose one unit of charisma for every move you make. You start with five arrows and no money. You can by more charisma or arrows if you find dragons gold or kill a dragon. At the start and periodically during the game you will be shown a map of every things location.
