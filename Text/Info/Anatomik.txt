Anatomik is a biology educational game.

While strolling in the park, player finds poor boy, who lost all of his internal organs. In order to help, they head into nearest hospital, where doctor agrees to restore everything. But with one condition: find eight organs on every of six floors and place them in proper place on body scheme. Also, each floor has a doctor with three questions, which lets player pass further or not, depending on answers. After completing all tasks, the door to next level opens.

Gameplay is simple arcade, with jump-kills and various bonus to pick up, like pogo stick or insecticide spray.

The most important part of the game is anatomic atlas, with rich articles about human body, illustrations and interactive schemes, which player is encouraged to use.
