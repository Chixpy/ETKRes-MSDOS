Zombies have taken over the world and as a commander of Special Forces it"s your mission to rescue humanity. Last Rites is a mission-based Doom clone and settled in the future providing you an arsenal of hi-tech weapons. The game"s 3D-Engine is on Doom level and has the ability to create large outside and inside levels.One special thing about Ocean Software"s game is, that in at least half the missions you have a team of four other commandos with you, who give you covering fire and help you fight the zombies.The game was only released in Europe.
