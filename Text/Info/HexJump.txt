Arranged on a monitor which came from the ancient board game, puzzle, known in Europe as the Solitaire. The board consists of thirty-seven round cells. All but one are occupied balls. The player must move one of the balls in the empty box, but you can only move in one. In this case the ball, stepped through which, removed from the board. The task - to remove the maximum number of balls. Definitely recommended to all those who fancies himself an intellectual. Try it.
