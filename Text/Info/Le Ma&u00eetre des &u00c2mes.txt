Le Ma\u00eetre des \u00c2mes is a fantasy role-playing game. The game will lead you through dungeons, secret chambers, tunnels...It tends to respect as much as possible the basic rules of famous RPG games.

At the beginning you have to create a party of 4 characters among 6 different ones: Warrior, Wizard, Cleric, Robber, Dwarf & Elf. Each one has special skills and attributes.

The whole story takes place in a castle. Kharram Akkad, Royal wizard, was on the verge of making a big discover but then vanished mysteriously. Since then, a huge storm has surrounded the place making all life disappear. A party of adventurers standing in front of the fortress decide to enter...
